# Cookie Clicker (Shortcuts Edition)
A stripped-down clone of [Cookie Clicker](https://cookieclicker.com/) created using the Shortcuts app for iOS.

## Why does this exist?
Because I got a phone recently and my mom won't let me install games on it. So I responded how any sane person would:

![Fine, I'll do it myself.](https://i.imgflip.com/85pc8o.jpg)

## Notes
- I made this in a total of like two and a half hours of haphazardly throwing code blocks together, so although I've yet to encounter any, do expect bugs.
- Requires two files to already exist in `On This iPhone/cookieclicker/sav`, those being `0.txt` and `1.txt`. `0.txt` is responsible for how many cookies you have, while `1.txt` is responsible for how many cookies you get with each click. I don't know how to check if they already exist and create them if they don't, so it's up to you, the end-user.
- Only five items are currently available for purchase, those being the Cursor (10 cookies), the Grandma (100 cookies), the Farm (500 cookies), the Factory (1,000 cookies), and the Bank (5,000 cookies). They each increase your cookies per click by 1/5th of their cost.
- Oh right, forgot to mention: maybe Shortcuts doesn't support it, or maybe I'm just really stupid, but items you purchase do not passively increase your cookie count like in the original game. Instead, they actively increase the amount of cookies you get each click. This is because I have no idea how to do the original game's passive method, either because it's not possible or because I'm an idiot.
- I do plan to add more items (and maybe also upgrades, eventually) as I get bored with what I have, but I refuse to commit to a schedule for that.
